Honey Trap - Southwark Park Gallery - 2023

LCD Screen, Frame, Camera, Mac Mini

A sickeningly sweet data harvest—wrapped in gold, dripping in glamour. It captures images of its viewers, offering them a fleeting compliment on their appearance. A simple exchange, yet beneath the surface, it exposes our quiet reliance on malicious systems for validation.
