Cuckcoo project

This multimedia project consisting ofYour data is being harvested and used to impersonate you, or even assimilated into merged AI entities. This phenomenon, where fragments of our online presence are combined, is becoming increasingly prevalent. To feed this insatiable data beast, we often fall into the trap of consuming content and freely giving away data points, thereby nurturing our digital amalgamations. 3D scan of The cuckcoo I aimed to confront the viewer with this truth in a way that was personal, profound, and unique. I wanted to take them on a journey. The method I used was to let them speak on the phone, unsure if they were speaking to anyone real, leading them through conversational beats. When enough data had been collected from their voice, a machine learning clone would kick in and start speaking back to them in their own voice, mannerisms, and intonation. Once the viewer hangs up, the end goal is reached. Two 1x1m metal sheets vibrate, producing sound and acting as giant speakers, letting them hear their voice reading a poem
